All West Driving Academy

We have the qualifications and ability to assess your current driving level. Our aim is to help you be a confident and competent driver to pass your test.

With many years experience driving Aucklands busy roads, we will coach you on how to drive safely and confidently. We specialise in driver education for teenagers to mature drivers.

At AW Driving Academy we provide quality driving instruction all over West Auckland. Our driving academy cars have security cameras installed. This ensures that our students are in the safest hands possible.

You will Learn to drive in a modern dual controlled car with independence but the knowledge of safety.
